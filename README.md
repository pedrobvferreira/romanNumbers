# romanNumbers
Convert any Roman number to Integer

Roman numerals
I: 1
V: 5
X: 10
L: 50
C: 100
D: 500
M: 1000


Examples:
VI: 6 (5 + 1 = 6)
LXX: 70 (50 + 10 + 10 = 70)
MCC: 1200 (1000 + 100 + 100 = 1200)
IV: 4 (5 – 1 = 4)
XC: 90 ( 100 – 10 = 90)
CM: 900 (1000 – 100 = 900)
XCV: XC + V or 90 + 5 = 95
XCIX XC + IX or 90 + 9 = 99
XX: 20
XXX: 30
